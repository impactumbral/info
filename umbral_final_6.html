.why now

The debate
is happening
without you.

Somewhere, right now, an algorithm is deciding what you see next.

The conversation about that decision — who controls it, who it affects, what it means for democracy, for identity, for the planet — is happening without you.

UMBRAL is a space to stand inside that question.

looking at

inside it.

.the experience

Three spaces.
One thread.

High-level technology installations, data visualization, immersive experiences. Each space takes one dimension of our world and puts technology under the lens.

01
SPACE 01

Gender

How do algorithms encode bias? Who does technology see — and who does it erase? An immersive exploration of the gendered architectures of digital systems.

02
SPACE 02

Climate

Technology as both cause and solution. Data at scale. What does the climate crisis look like when you can truly see it — with your own hands?

03
SPACE 03

Democracy

Disinformation, surveillance, digital rights. What happens to democratic debate when the infrastructure of public life is privately owned?

Impacto Digital works at the intersection of technology, society, and human rights. We build projects that create space for the conversations that matter.

UMBRAL is not an exhibition about technology. It's an exhibition about us — about what we choose to do with it.

Universities, research centers, civil society organizations and tech companies who want to co-create the exhibition and shape its content.

03 ——

Companies who want to associate their name with a rigorous, independent project that reaches researchers, educators, policy makers and the general public.

Museums, cultural centers and institutions interested in hosting UMBRAL. We're looking for spaces that believe in the power of experiential, participatory culture.
